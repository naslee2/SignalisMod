# SignalisMod

A silly mod for Starsector that adds the Eusan Nation to the base game. May or may not have space lesbian love. May or may not have space DDR. Who knows?

Requires LazyLib, MagicLib and Nexerlin

Get them:
LazyLib https://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=5444.0
MagicLib https://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=25868.0
Nexerlin https://fractalsoftworks.com/forum/index.php?topic=9175.0
